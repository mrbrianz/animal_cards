Penguins

Penguins seem to have no special fear of humans, and have approached groups of explorers without hesitation.

  • Largest Species: Emperor Penguin
  • Number of Species: 18-20
  • Scientific Name: Spheniscidae
  • Lifespan: 20 Years

Penguins (order Sphenisciformes, family Spheniscidae) are a group of aquatic, flightless birds living almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere, especially in Antarctica. Source: Wikipedia
